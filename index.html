Helpful Articles

Why Use Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML gives meaning to your content. Using the right tags helps browsers and assistive tools understand how your page is structured.

Making Websites More Accessible

HTML has built-in tools to help people with disabilities. Tags like <header>, <nav>, and <main> help screen readers navigate your content more easily.
